Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • meat
  • cheese
  • sauce

Steps

  1. Boil your water then add your noodles and cook for 11 minutes
  2. While noodles are cooking, brown meat in medium sized sauce pan
  3. In a 9x13 glass pan layer cooked noodles, meat, sauce, and cheese. Starting with sauce first.
  4. Cook for 30 minutes in a pre-heated oven at 350 degrees
  5. Serve hot, top with additional cheese if preferred.
