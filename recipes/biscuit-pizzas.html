Biscuit Pizzas

Ingredients

  • Your favorite canned biscuits
  • Your favorite pizza toppings
  • 8oz of mozzarella cheese
  • canned pizza sauce

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit
  2. Roll pre-made biscuits to approximately 1/4 inch thick each
  3. After rolling add sauce, cheese, and toppings to each mini pizza
  4. Place mini pizzas on top of a baking sheet
  5. Cook mini pizzas in oven for approximately 25-30 minutes, or until biscuits have finished cooking
  6. Serve warm with additional sauce if preferred
